I have been in the profession for over 25 years and have had the incredible opportunity to complement my rich clinical experience in a leading clinic in Bulgaria with theoretical, academic knowledge and global innovations in the field of rehabilitation and medicine. I follow the latest methodologies and apply them through the lens of physiology, biomechanics, and neurology, based on the individual characteristics of each patient. The secret to resolving a patient's pain lies in an individualized approach, combined with the trust between us. Since rehabilitation supports natural recovery, creating and following a specialized recovery program is crucial for the success of the treatment. I hold a PhD in kinesitherapy on the topic of chronic lower back pain. My interests include disorders of the musculoskeletal system (MSK), peripheral nervous system (PNS), musculoskeletal dysfunctions, pain syndromes, scoliosis, and pre- and post-operative conditions. In medicine, there are three main approaches to restoring health:
- Through natural physical factors (rehabilitation, physiotherapy)
- Medication
- Surgical intervention
The informed decision of the patient regarding all possible treatment options is their choice.
