Details

I have been in the profession for 25+ years and have the incredible opportunity to complement my extensive clinical experience in a leading clinic in Bulgaria with theoretical, academic knowledge and global innovations in the field of rehabilitation and medicine. I follow the latest methodologies and apply them through the knowledge of physiology, biomechanics, and neurology based on the individual characteristics of the patient. The individual approach is the secret to solving the patient's pain in combination with the trust between us. Since rehabilitation supports natural recovery, developing and following a specialized recovery program is important for the success of the treatment. I have a PhD in kinesitherapy on the topic of chronic low back pain. My interests include diseases of the musculoskeletal system (MSD), peripheral nervous system (PNS), musculoskeletal dysfunctions, pain syndromes, scoliosis, pre- and post-operative conditions. In medicine, there are three main approaches to restoring health:
- Through natural factors (rehabilitation, physiotherapy)
- Medicinally
- Surgically
The informed decision of the patient about all possible treatment options is their choice.
